Airport Delays Made Clear

Enhancing Passenger Communication with Clear and Friendly Messaging

Flight Delayed Messages

Flight details can be overwhelming and unclear during delays. With a friendly tone and simple words, this project shows how better messages can ease stress for passengers.

Scenario

Marco is waiting for his daughter's delayed flight at the airport. He feels stressed and wants to know the flight's status.

Problem: Flight delays cause stress and uncertainty.

User Goal: Gain accurate updates and reassurance.

Business Goal: Provide clear information to improve passenger satisfaction.

Proposed Solution

Flight updates often feel impersonal and cold, ignoring the doubts and stress passengers experience. By integrating a friendly tone and clear steps, we help passengers feel informed and reassured.

Message: We're sorry for the delay to your flight. Please continue to monitor the airport screens and check Flight Tracker for updates.
