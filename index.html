Flight Delayed Messages

Flight details can be overwhelming and unclear during delays. With a friendly tone and simple words, this project shows how better messages can ease stress for passengers.

Scenario

Marco, a passenger waiting for his daughter's delayed flight, feels stressed and confused due to lack of updates. Clear, empathetic communication can help him feel supported.

Problem: Flight delays cause stress and uncertainty.

User Goal: Gain accurate updates and reassurance.

Business Goal: Provide clear information to improve passenger satisfaction.
