Airport Delays Made Clear

Enhancing Passenger Communication with Clear and Friendly Messaging

Flight Delayed Messages

Flight details can be overwhelming and unclear during delays. With a friendly tone and simple words, this project shows how better messages can ease stress for passengers.

Scenario

Marco, a passenger waiting for his daughter's delayed flight, feels stressed and confused due to lack of updates. Clear, empathetic communication can help him feel supported.

Problem: Flight delays cause stress and uncertainty.

User Goal: Gain accurate updates and reassurance.

Business Goal: Provide clear information to improve passenger satisfaction.

User-Centered Design Insights

Scenario Content Description Content Element Rationale
Marco needs to pick his daughter at the airport, but her flight is delayed. Marco doesn't know what to do and feels stressed. He would like to know more about the condition of the flight his daughter is on. Apology and suggest a solution to alleviate the stress.

We are sorry about the 'time' delayed due to 'reason' in the airport of departure. We are here to help.

Button #1: Flight Status

Button #2: Customer Support
  • The flight status button gives the User a sense of control over the situation.
  • The customer support button helps the User feel less alone in a situation of stress.

Proposed Solution

Flight updates often feel impersonal and cold, ignoring the stress and doubts of passengers. Clear, empathetic messaging such as "We're sorry for the delay to your flight. Please monitor the airport screens and check the Flight Tracker for updates" can provide clarity and reassurance.
